Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report an issue? ▼

Click "Report Issue" on the home page to fill out a quick form with details about the problem, including the location, type of issue, and any photos if available.

How long does it take to resolve issues? ▼

Response time varies by issue type and location. High-priority issues (like fallen poles) are typically addressed within hours, while maintenance issues may take longer. You'll receive updates via SMS.

Will I receive SMS updates? ▼

Yes! When you report an issue, you'll receive SMS updates at key stages. Make sure your phone number is correctly entered when submitting your report.

Is my personal information safe? ▼

Yes, we take privacy seriously. Your information is only shared with relevant utility companies when needed to resolve your issue.

🚨 Emergency Issues

For immediate emergencies that pose safety risks:

  • Fallen power poles or damaged lines: Call REG immediately at 1212
  • Major water leaks/burst pipes: Call WASAC at 1234
  • Gas leaks or other hazards: Call emergency services at 112
