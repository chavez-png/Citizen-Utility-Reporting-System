ℹ️ What Happens Next?

  1. Confirmation: You'll receive an SMS confirming your report was received
  2. Review: Our admin team will review and categorize your report
  3. Alert: Relevant utility company will be notified immediately
  4. Updates: You'll receive SMS updates as the issue progresses
  5. Resolution: Once fixed, you'll receive a final confirmation

⏱️ Note: High-priority issues are typically addressed within 2-4 hours. Standard issues may take 24-48 hours.
